What we offer

Thoughtful care, from routine to restorative.

Whether it's a check-up for your child or a smile you've been thinking about for years, we're here with an honest conversation and a clear plan.

Exams & cleanings

Comprehensive check-ups with low-radiation digital X-rays and gentle hygiene visits.

Composite fillings

Tooth-colored fillings that blend naturally — no metal, no compromise on strength.

Teeth whitening

Professional KöR whitening — dramatic, even results with the care your enamel deserves.

Porcelain veneers

Hand-matched veneers to refine chips, staining, and gaps into a smile that still feels like yours.

Crowns & bridges

Natural-looking restorations that bring back strength, shape, and confidence.

Implant restoration

Lifelike implant crowns and bridges — permanent solutions that feel like your own teeth.

Extractions

Gentle, precise extractions when they're truly needed — with a calm plan for aftercare.

Digital diagnostics

Low-radiation digital X-rays and intraoral imaging for early, precise treatment planning.

Emergency care

Dental emergencies can't wait. Call our emergency line and we'll take care of you.
